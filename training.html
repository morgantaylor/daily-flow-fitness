Training Programs

Sports Specific & Performance Enhancement Pain Management & Mobility Post Rehab & Injury
Sport Specific & Performance Enhancement:

This training program is perfect for anyone looking to improve on their endurance, strength, power, and or agility in a specific skill, sport, or activity. Whether you are an aspiring professional athlete or just trying to hold on to the last bit of athleticism you have, I can help. With an emphasis on biomechanics and form we can get you or keep you where you want to be.

Phase 1

In the begining we'll focus on building your foundation through stability, core, corrective exercise and cardio. Some people progress faster than others and some people start with a higher level base fitness.

Phase 2

This is where we'll start to add in some heavier weights, working to strengthen the body as a whole while still working on phase 1 to fight any imbalances or compensations.

Phase 3

Power, speed and agility is the main focus at this point. Fast pace, all out, honing your skills in a newly balanced, stronger version of yourself.

By blending sport specific/functional movements, a little power lifting with core conditioning , breathing and massage we aim to increase strength, power, reaction time, proprioception and learn to increase your focus while giving maximal effort. High school, college, pro or amateurm Daily Flow Fitness can help you condition your mind, body, and awareness creating a potent combination that will boosts your performance.

Pain Management & Mobility:

If you struggle with chronic pains, aches, and every day tasks have become difficult I can help. Employing proven mobility techniques, combined with corrective exercise and a basic strengthening program we can restore full ranges of motion. All these sessions end with 10-15 minutes of mobility or massage to bring your body full circle.

Phase 1

To start with we will work on correcting imbalances to help rebuild and improve the way you cary yourself and your posture while improving mobility.

Phase 2

In the next segment we'll build off of correcting your imbalances by building up your core and stability. Your core and stability are critical to maining flow in your daily life.

Phase 3

In our last phase we'll take everything we've covered and really apply it to how you use your core in everyday movements. Whether you sit all day at a desk or you're on your feet all day this program will leave you feeling balanced and ready to take on those everyday movements that you've been dreading.

Post Rehab & Injury:

If you're a little freaked out about getting back to normal activity but have been cleared by your doctor or physical therapist this program is geared towards you. After an injury, surgery, or rehab it can be difficult to get back to feeling your best.

Don’t let that nagging injury stand in your way. You may have developed imbalances and compensations, or it could be psychological. Through my extensive experience, personally and professionally, we can start to build you a stable foundation so you can be active again.

What we'll work on:

Regulating the CNS (Central nervous system) through specific breathing patterns and becoming reconnect with parts of your body that have remained dormant for quite some time.

We will build your confidence by incorporating core movements with mobility and reestablish a strong and health foundation.

All sessions end with 10-15 minutes of mobility and/or massage
