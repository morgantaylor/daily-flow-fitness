Cole Citret

Founder of Daily Flow Fitness

Specialties: Performance enhancement, biomecanics, pain management

The unusual blend of circumstances that led Cole to the fitness world gives him a truly unique perspective in the health and wellness field. Not taking health and fitness seriously early on he found himself continuously dealing with injuries. Each injury was an important lesson learned the hard way. After 3 ACL injuries in (’07,’09,’14), a 5th degree A/C separation (’07), and 2 concussions Cole finally decided to follow a path of health, fitness, and helping others. Cole is passionate about helping others reach their full potential and gaining awareness of their body through flow. Through the major experiences in Coles life he developed a passion for the human body and its potential.

Certifications:

CMT,NMT certified massage therapist since 2011
NASM - CPT,CES 2011/2013
M|WOD certified 101/102 coach
