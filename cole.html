Cole Citret CMT, NMT, CPT

Founder of Daily Flow Fitness

Expertise: Performance enhancement, biomechanics, pain management

The blend of circumstances that led me to the fitness world provides a unique perspective to the health and wellness field. Not taking health and fitness seriously, early on, I found himself continuously dealing with injuries. Each injury was an important lesson learned the hard way. After 3 ACL injuries in (’07,’09,’14), a 5th degree A/C separation (’07), and 2 concussions, I finally decided to follow a path of health, fitness, and helping others. I'm my clients reaching their full potential and gaining awareness of their bodies through "flow".

Certifications:

CMT: Certified Massage Therapist since 2011
NMT: Neuromuscular Massage Therapist
NASM (National Academy of Sports Medicine):
 CPT (Certified Personal Trainer)

Experience:
