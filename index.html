No Pain No Gain?

Forget everything you've heard about no pain no gain in fitness. It's an antiquated approach to health and wellness that has consumed media and marketing for the past 50 years. So much chronic pain and disfunction can be improved or even resolved with a relatively conservative and cognitive fitness program.

A lot of science has come out about training the body smarter. Let Daily Flow Fitness help you find your daily flow with the effective approach I have developed over years of working with countless clients with specific needs.
